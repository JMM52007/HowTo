Chocolate Chip Zucchini Bread

yeild: 1 loaf or 12 muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup canola or vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cuppack light or dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg, at room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup grated zucchini*

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Grease a 9x5(or 8x4) inch loaf pan. See notes for muffins.
  2. Whisk the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and chocolate chips togetherin a large bowl until toroughly combined. Set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk the oil, brown sugar, granulated sugar, egg, vanilla, and zucchinitogether until combined.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Gently whisk until *just* combined; do not overmix. Batter will be semi-thick.
  5. Spread the batter into prepared loaf pan. Bake for 45-55 minutes. Baking times vary so keep a eye on yours. The bread is done when a toothpick inserted in the center comes out *mostly* clean with zero raw batter.
  6. Remove the bread from the oven and set on a wire rack. Allow to cool completely before slicing.
  7. Cover and store leftover bread at room temperature for up to 5 days.

Make ahead tip:Make the bread ahead of time by freezing it. Bake it, cool it, wrap it up in aluminum foil and freeze the loaf as a whole. Allow to thaw overnight in the refridgerator and bring to room temperature before slicing and serving. Freezes weel up to 3 months.

Recipe Notes

*Very lightly blot the zucchini. Do not squeeze moisture out, just give it a light blot to rid some, especially if your zucchini is extrs wet.

Zucchini Bread Muffins: Grease a 12-count muffin pan or line with liners. Prepare batter in step 2. Spoon the batter evenly into each liner, filingeach all the way to the top. Bake the muffins for 5 minutes at 425F then , keeping tha muffins in the oven, reduce the oven temperatureto 350F. Bake for an additional 13-16 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. The total time these muffins take in the oven is about 18-21 minutes, give or take. Allow the muffins to cool for 5 minutes in the mufiin pan, then transfer to a wirerack to continue cooling.

Bread

Classic White Bread

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 cups warm water (baby bottle temperature)
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoon active dry yeast (not instant)
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 Tablespoon salt
  • 7 1/2 to 8 1/2 cups bread flour

Directions

  1. Combine sugar, warm water, and yeast in a large mixing bowl. (Or a Bosch mixer is perfect for this recipe)
  2. Let stand for 10 minutes while the yeast proofs and dissolves.
  3. Add oil, egg, and salt into yeast mixture. Mix to combine.
  4. Add flour, one cup at a time, mixing well and kneading well in between. Don't add all the flour at once, especislly towards the end. How much flour you need depends on lots of variables- you don't want dough that's too dry. It needs to be elastic and slightly sticky, but not so wet that it leaves moisture on the side of the bowl.
  5. Knead dough for 5 minutes by hand or 2-3 mu=inutes in a mixer.
  6. Cover dough with a clean dish towel and allow it to rise util doubled, about 45 min. Then punch down. allow it to rise for a 2nd time, covered, for 30-45 min.
  7. Separate dough into 3 equal sections, or for mini loaves separate each of those 3rds into 3rds again(9 total) and with a rolling pin, roll into sections into a rectangle. With each section, roll it from bottom to top and put seam side down into a greased mini loaf pan. (Or if making 3 larger loaves, use a 4.5x8.5 inch loaf pan). Cover with a towel and let rise for 30 minutes or until doubled.
  8. Bake at 380F for 20 minutes for mini's, or 24 minutes for larger loaves. Take out of loaf pans, rub tops with butter, and let cool on wire rack for at least 30 minutes before slicing.
  9. ENJOY!

Mallowmelt

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Marshmellows(divided)
  • 2 cups Chocolate Chips(divided)
  • 2 cups Butterscotch Chips(divided)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 2/3 cups Brown Sugar
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • 2 teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 14oz Sweetened and Condensed Milk
  • 1/2 cup butter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease a 9×13 inch metal baking pan.
  3. Using a microwave-safe bowl, melt 1 cup of the butterscotch chips with the butter in the microwave. It’s easiest to work in 20 second intervals, stirring in between. Set aside to cool. (Note: Do not over melt! It’s okay if a few lumps remain.)
  4. In a large bowl, stir the brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla until combined.
  5. In a small bowl, stir the flour, baking powder and salt until combined.
  6. When the butterscotch mixture has cooled, pour into the large bowl and stir to combine with the brown sugar/egg/vanilla mixture. Add the flour mixture and stir until just mixed.
  7. Stir in 2 cups of the marshmallows, 1 cup of chocolate chips and 1/2 cup of the butterscotch chips.
  8. Spread the batter evenly into the prepared baking pan.
  9. Top with the remaining marshmallows, chocolate, and butterscotch chips, scattering evenly.
  10. Drizzle entire can of sweetened & condensed milk evenly over the top.
  11. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until set. Top will be browned but still quite gooey.
  12. Cool, completely (at least 2-3 hours) and serve!

Chocolate-Mint Ripplefluffs

Yield: 10-12 Ripplefluffs

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/3 cup cocoa
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Chocolate mint candies (Andies mints recommended)
  • Chocolate sprinkles

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Line cupcake pan with foil liners (paper will work, but brownies tend to stick).
  3. In large mixing bowl, combine melted butter, both sugars, vanilla and beaten eggs until smooth.
  4. In separate bowl combine the flour, cocoa, salt and baking powder.
  5. Fold dry ingredients into wet ingredients.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop by 1/4 cup into prepared cupcake pan.
  8. Press 1 chocolate mint candy into the center of each and smooth batter over the top.
  9. Top with sprinkles.
  10. Bake 350 for 20-25 minutes.
  11. Remove from oven and place a second chocolate mint candy on top.
  12. Let cool 10 minutes then remove from pan to cooling rack and allow to cool completely.

Butter-Toffee Ripplefluffs

Yield: 12-15 Ripplefluffs

Ingredients

  • 12 tbsp ( 1 1/2 sticks ) unsalted butter melted and slightly cooled
  • 1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs beaten
  • 4 tsp vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup toffee pieces (for those with nut allergies–smashed Werther’s Original hard candies work perfectly)
  • 15 chocolate kisses
  • Rainbow Sprinkles

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Line cupcake pans with foil (paper works, but will stick more).
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Add beaten eggs and vanilla and mix well.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt.
  6. Fold dry ingredients into wet ingredients until just combined.
  7. Fold in white chocolate chips and toffee pieces.
  8. Scoop by 1/4 cup into prepared pan.
  9. Press a chocolate kiss into the center of each and smooth batter over the top.
  10. Add sprinkles.
  11. Bake 350 for 20-25 minutes.
  12. Let cool in pan 10 minutes then remove to cooling rack.
